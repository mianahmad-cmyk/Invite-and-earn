Refund Policy

Please review our refund policy before proceeding with any transactions on Invite & Earn.

  • 1. Refunds are only applicable in cases of technical errors or duplicate payments.
  • 2. Refund requests must be submitted within 24 hours of the transaction.
  • 3. Withdrawals of earned balance are not eligible for refunds.
  • 4. Users must provide payment proof and transaction ID for refund processing.
  • 5. Refunds will be processed through the same payment method used for the transaction.
  • 6. Processing time for refunds is 7-10 business days.
  • 7. The 50 PKR signup fee is non-refundable under standard circumstances.
  • 8. In case of payment gateway issues, contact our support team immediately.
  • 9. Chargebacks initiated by the user will result in account suspension.
  • 10. Refund policies are subject to change based on business requirements.
