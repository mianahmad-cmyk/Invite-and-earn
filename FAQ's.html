FAQs

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Invite & Earn.

  • 1. What is the signup fee for Invite-and-Earn? The signup fee is 50 PKR, which is split between the admin and the referrer.
  • 2. Can I get a refund after paying the signup fee? No, the signup fee is non-refundable under standard circumstances.
  • 3. How can I withdraw my earnings? You can request a withdrawal once you have a minimum balance of 50 PKR.
  • 4. What payment methods are accepted? Currently, we accept payments via Stripe. More options will be added soon.
  • 5. How long does it take to process a withdrawal? Withdrawals are processed within 7-10 business days after verification.
  • 6. Can I change my referrer after signing up? No, the referrer cannot be changed once the signup process is complete.
  • 7. What if my payment fails but the amount is deducted? Contact support with payment proof, and we will assist you.
  • 8. Can I delete my account? Yes, you can request account deletion by contacting support.
  • 9. How is my data protected? We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect user data.
  • 10. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue? Report any technical issues to support, and we will resolve them promptly.
