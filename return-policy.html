Return Policy

Please read our return policy carefully before making any transactions on Invite & Earn.

  • 1. All payments made during the signup process are non-refundable.
  • 2. Once a user successfully registers and pays the 50 PKR signup fee, the payment cannot be reversed.
  • 3. In case of duplicate payments, users can contact support for a refund within 24 hours.
  • 4. Refunds will only be processed in case of technical errors or system malfunctions.
  • 5. If the payment fails but the amount is deducted, contact support with payment proof.
  • 6. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after verification.
  • 7. Accurate account information must be provided to avoid delays in refunds.
  • 8. The 50 PKR fee is split between the admin and the referrer; hence, refund requests are evaluated carefully.
  • 9. Withdrawals of earned balance are not subject to refunds.
  • 10. We reserve the right to deny refund requests that do not comply with our policy.
